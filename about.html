Built On A Promise

Your total satisfaction is our cardinal concern

Cardinal Roofing & Exteriors was founded in Round Rock in 2009. Owner and CEO John Clampitt spent years as a contractor and sales manager across Central Texas, gaining deep knowledge in roofing and home improvement before starting the company.

Today Cardinal is run by the Clampitt family, and that shows up in the work. We treat your home like it belongs to a neighbor, because in Central Texas it usually does. We use the highest quality materials, communicate clearly, and finish efficiently.
